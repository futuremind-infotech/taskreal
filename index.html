Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries about Bittangala Woods

What is the possession timeline?

The project is scheduled for completion by Q2 2026. We have a proven track record of on-time delivery with 98% of our projects handed over as per schedule. You can expect possession within 18 months from booking.

What are the payment plans available?

We offer flexible payment plans including construction-linked plans (CLP) and down payment plans. Home loan assistance is available from all major banks with attractive interest rates. EMI options start from as low as ₹68,500/month for 2BHK units.

Is the project RERA approved?

Yes, Bittangala Woods is fully RERA approved and compliant with all regulatory requirements. Our RERA registration number is available on request, and all project details are transparently shared with buyers.

What is the resale value potential?

The area has shown consistent appreciation of 12-15% annually over the past 5 years. With upcoming infrastructure projects including metro expansion and IT park developments, we expect strong appreciation potential of 40-50% over the next 5 years.

What amenities are included in maintenance?

Monthly maintenance covers all common area amenities including swimming pool, gym, clubhouse, landscaping, security, power backup, and water supply. The estimated maintenance charge is ₹3-4 per sq.ft based on unit size.

Can I customize my unit interiors?

Yes, we offer customization options for interiors including flooring, kitchen fittings, bathroom fixtures, and paint colors. Our design team will work with you to create your dream home within the structural guidelines.

What is the parking allocation?

Each unit comes with one covered parking space. Additional parking can be purchased at ₹3.5 lakhs per space. Visitor parking is available in common areas with ample space for guests.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

We offer a transparent cancellation policy as per RERA guidelines. Refunds are processed within 45 days of cancellation request. Detailed terms and conditions are provided in the booking agreement.
